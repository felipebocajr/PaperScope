How to Use PaperScope

Each paper displayed has been curated and ranked by artificial intelligence based on innovation, impact, methodology, and topic relevance.

  1. Select a Week: Choose an extraction period from the left panel to see papers from that week.
  2. Pick a Topic: Select a research category (Agentic AI, LLMs, Computer Vision, etc.) to view the top paper.
  3. Read & Explore: Read the AI-curated summary. Click underlined terms to view definitions in the glossary sidebar.
  4. Share Feedback: Let us know what you think to help improve Paper Rank.
